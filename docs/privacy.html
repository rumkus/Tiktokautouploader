Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

TikTok Auto Uploader collects minimal information necessary to function:

  • OAuth Tokens: Authentication tokens provided by TikTok to authorize video uploads. These are stored locally on your device only.
  • Video Metadata: File names and descriptions you provide for uploads. This data is only used to publish content to TikTok and is not stored beyond the upload process.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your TikTok username or password
  • Personal identification information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Video content (videos are sent directly to TikTok's servers)

3. Data Storage

All data (configuration and tokens) is stored locally on your computer. No data is sent to any server other than TikTok's official API endpoints.

4. Third-Party Services

The App interacts exclusively with TikTok's official API (open.tiktokapis.com). Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy, available at tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Data Deletion

To remove all data associated with the App, simply delete the project folder from your computer. You can also revoke access from your TikTok account settings under "Manage app permissions".

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by TikTok in your jurisdiction).

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
